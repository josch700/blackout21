Blackout on March 21st – Everyone can help to save the internet!

The free internet and all it's platforms are in danger, so you can also help to save the web! German Wikipedia shutting down it's entire site on March 21st to inform everyone on the new EU Copyright directive.

What can I do on March 21st?

Everyone

Please upload just a black avatar as profile image on your social platforms. So you can show everyone, what a possible upload filter could affect!

Website owners / Bloggers

Just publish a black page to inform your visitors about the European copyright reforms. You can find integrations here.

Streamers

You can't stream just a black screen, but you can talk about all the protests on this day! You can find related banners and more here.
